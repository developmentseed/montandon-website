# Partners & Governance

Montandon is a collaborative effort, bringing together organizations with expertise in humanitarian data, software engineering, Earth observation, and disaster response.

See the [Governance](/governance) page for details on project governance.

## Our Partners

<div style="display: flex; align-items: center; gap: 12px; margin-bottom: 8px;">
  <img src="/logos/icons/ifrc-icon.svg" alt="" style="max-height: 28px; width: 28px;" />
  <h3 style="margin: 0;">IFRC</h3>
</div>

The [International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC)](https://www.ifrc.org/) is the world's largest humanitarian network. IFRC is the founding sponsor of Montandon, hosting the platform under the IFRC GO ecosystem to support global disaster response with reliable, standardized data.

<div style="display: flex; align-items: center; gap: 12px; margin-bottom: 8px;">
  <img src="/logos/icons/devseed-icon.svg" alt="" style="max-height: 28px; width: 28px;" />
  <h3 style="margin: 0;">Development Seed</h3>
</div>

[Development Seed](https://developmentseed.org/) is an implementing partner and the technical lead for Montandon. They bring deep expertise in geospatial data infrastructure, STAC, and open-source development to build and maintain the Montandon platform.

<div style="display: flex; align-items: center; gap: 12px; margin-bottom: 8px;">
  <img src="/logos/icons/togglecorp-icon.svg" alt="" style="max-height: 28px; width: 28px;" />
  <h3 style="margin: 0;">ToggleCorp</h3>
</div>

[ToggleCorp](https://togglecorp.com/) is an implementing partner, contributing software development expertise to the Montandon project. Their work focuses on data processing, ETL pipelines, and building the tools that power Montandon's data integration.

<div style="display: flex; align-items: center; gap: 12px; margin-bottom: 8px;">
  <img src="/logos/icons/mapaction-icon.png" alt="" style="max-height: 28px; width: 28px;" />
  <h3 style="margin: 0;">MapAction</h3>
</div>

[MapAction](https://mapaction.org/) is a humanitarian mapping charity that provides expertise in disaster mapping and spatial data. They contribute domain knowledge and operational insights to ensure Montandon meets the real-world needs of humanitarian responders.

<div style="display: flex; align-items: center; gap: 12px; margin-bottom: 8px;">
  <img src="/logos/full/esa.png" alt="" style="max-height: 28px; max-width: 28px;" />
  <h3 style="margin: 0;">European Space Agency (ESA)</h3>
</div>

The [European Space Agency (ESA)](https://www.esa.int/) is a partner in the Montandon project, contributing funding for the response portfolio of data. ESA's support enables Montandon to soon integrate operational response data.
